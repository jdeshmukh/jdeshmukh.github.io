  • [Oct '20] Co-Chair of RV 2020 with Dejan Nickovic. Had a fun virtual conference, but missing in-person conferences even more now!
  • [Sep '20] Received new CPS Medium grant for doing research on 'Spatio-Temporal Logics for Analyzing and Querying Perception Systems.' PI Georgios Fainekos @ ASU is the boss! Looking forward to the collaboration with Georgios and Yezhou Yang. May our research prove useful!
  • [Jul '20] Paper on clairvoyant monitoring accepted at FORMATS 2020 (Kudos to Xin for all her hard work)
  • [Jul '20] Paper on mining shape expressions accepted at EMSOFT 2020 (Thanks to the Viennese collaborators Ezio, Dejan, Cristi & Felix!)
